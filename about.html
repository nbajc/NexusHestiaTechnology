The Founders

Co-Founder & CEO · Chief Strategist

Natasha Bajc

Architect · AI Researcher · Educator · Multimedia Artist

Natasha is an interdisciplinary architect, computational designer, and AI researcher whose practice dissolves the boundary between built space and intelligent systems. With graduate degrees from SCI-Arc (M.Arch, Algorithmic Design) and the University of Belgrade (M.Eng Architecture & Urbanism), and ongoing M.CS studies in Affective Computing at USC, she brings a rare synthesis of spatial intuition and machine intelligence to enterprise AI.

She has led design and technology teams across three continents — from Disneyland Tokyo to Caesars Palace Las Vegas — and founded multiple research and technology ventures: me NaN ce Institute, Volynt Inc., and Zetta AI. At Nexus Hestia, she directs the architecture of advanced behavioral analytics frameworks and personalized AI ecosystems for enterprise clients.

Natasha is a practicing multimedia artist, educator at Pasadena City College, TEDx speaker, and exhibiting artist at the 10th International Beijing Biennale 2025 and Venice Architecture Biennale 2018.
